Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Linqo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address and full name
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Username and display preferences
  • Authentication credentials

2.2 Virtual Currency Data (Stars)

  • Star purchase records and transaction history
  • Balance information (Purchased Stars and Earned Stars)
  • Spending history (content unlocks, gifts, reactions)
  • For creators: earning records and payout history

2.3 Creator & Monetization Data

  • Content upload records and approval status
  • Earnings from content sales
  • Withdrawal request details
  • Payment method information (crypto wallets, mobile banking)

2.4 Device & Usage Information

  • Device identifiers for push notifications
  • App usage analytics and session data
  • Content interaction patterns
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process Star purchases and maintain accurate balances
  • Facilitate content unlocking and gift transactions
  • Process creator earnings and withdrawal requests
  • Send push notifications for important updates
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or policy violations
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Virtual Currency (Stars) Privacy

Important: We maintain two separate balance types:

  • Purchased Stars: Stars bought via Google Play Billing. In-app use only; cannot be withdrawn.
  • Earned Stars: Stars earned via content sales/gifts/rewards; may be withdrawn by eligible creators.

Transaction records are stored securely and encrypted. Purchases are processed through Google Play's secure billing system.

5. Creator Monetization Data

  • Crypto wallet addresses (for cryptocurrency payouts)
  • Mobile banking details
  • Withdrawal history and payout records
  • Content performance analytics

Payment information is encrypted and used only to process withdrawal requests.

6. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) for database access control
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Regular security monitoring
  • Google Play Billing security for purchases

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google Play (Star purchases)
  • Payment processors (creator withdrawals)
  • Analytics providers (anonymized usage data)
  • Legal authorities (when required by law)

8. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us through the in-app support system.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Transaction records may be kept for up to 7 years for legal compliance. After account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days unless legally required otherwise.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify significant changes through the app or email. Continued use indicates acceptance.
